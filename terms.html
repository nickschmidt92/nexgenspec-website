  • The device is factory-reset, restored from blank, or sold/given to a new owner without an iCloud Backup having been created.

  • A bug in the app or in iOS results in data corruption (rare, but possible).

Inspector backup obligations. Inspectors are strongly advised to:

  • Enable iCloud Backup on the device used for NexGenSpec (iOS Settings → [your name] → iCloud → iCloud Backup → On).

  • Retain encrypted copies for the duration of any applicable retention obligations. The industry-standard window is five (5) years; consult your professional liability insurance carrier and licensing board for the specific obligations that apply in your jurisdiction.

Multi-device note. NexGenSpec inspections live on the device they were created on and do not sync between devices. An inspector with multiple iPads should treat each device as an independent silo. Use the export-to-Files feature to move records between devices intentionally.

Inspectors should not rely on a sent email as the sole long-term archive of an inspection. Always retain a separate backup copy.
