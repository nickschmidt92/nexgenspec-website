The math at 30 inspections a month

On Spectora’s Advanced tier: $109 + (30 × $4) = $229/month — about $2,748/year.

On NexGenSpec: $49/month — $588/year (or $449 billed annually). Same month of work, roughly $2,100+ a year difference. That’s a radon monitor, your E&O deductible, or a vacation.

Why local-first matters in this job

Your inspection photos, signatures, and reports are evidence. NexGenSpec keeps them on your device — not on someone else’s servers. The app works at full speed with zero bars in a basement, and if a finding is ever challenged, your audit trail of timestamps and acceptance logs is yours, on hardware you control. No cloud subscription expiring between you and your own records.

When Spectora is still the right call

Honesty over marketing: pick Spectora if you need Android (NexGenSpec is iOS/iPadOS), if you want desktop web-based report editing, or if you run a multi-inspector firm today — NexGenSpec Teams is still in development. If those don’t apply, you’re paying roughly 2–4× more for infrastructure you may not use.
