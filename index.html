Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

NexGenSpec ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address and password used to create your account. If you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID identifier and optional email.
  • Inspection Data: Client names, property addresses, inspection notes, photos, annotations, signatures, LiDAR scans, and videos you create within the app. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • Location Data: When you grant permission, we use your device location to auto-fill property addresses. Location is used only at the moment of the request and is not stored or tracked.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and crash logs are collected via Firebase Crashlytics to diagnose and fix issues.
  • Purchase Information: Subscription status is managed by Apple. We do not collect or store your payment details.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the core inspection workflow (reports, photos, annotations, signatures)
  • To generate PDF reports and invoices
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app stability
  • To verify your subscription entitlement

Data Storage and Security

Inspection data is stored locally on your device with iOS file protection enabled. We do not upload your inspection data, photos, or reports to our servers. Crash logs are sent to Firebase Crashlytics (Google) for diagnostics only.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication: Account sign-in
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reporting and diagnostics
  • Apple StoreKit: Subscription management and purchase verification

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

  • You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting contact@nexgenspec.com
  • You can deny or revoke location, camera, microphone, and photo library permissions at any time in iOS Settings
  • You can request a copy of any data we hold about you

Children's Privacy

NexGenSpec is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Common Questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Open your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. You can change or cancel your NexGenSpec subscription there. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.

How do I delete my account?

In the app, go to Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data and cannot be undone.

Which devices support LiDAR scanning?

LiDAR scanning requires an iPhone Pro or iPad Pro with a LiDAR sensor (iPhone 12 Pro and later, iPad Pro 2020 and later).

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Sign in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase your subscription. Your Pro access will be restored automatically. If not, use the "Restore Purchases" option on the upgrade screen.

Is my data backed up?

Inspection data is stored locally on your device and included in your standard iCloud/iTunes backups. We do not upload your inspection data to external servers.
