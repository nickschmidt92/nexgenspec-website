FAQ

Quick answers.

Do you ship?

Yes — order our R&R Kit and we deliver to your door. Local Colorado deliveries can come fully assembled.

Do you do custom sizes?

Yes. Most of our standard ramps come in multiple heights and widths, and we'll build custom dimensions on request.

Can you build at my house?

Yes. We do on-site backyard builds — mini ramps, half pipes, and complete setups.

What's the cheapest ramp you offer?

Our small Everyday Quarter Pipes start at $200. Fun Box combos start at $200 too.

How is your ramp better than a used one online?

Brand new. Skater designed and built. Locally made. Fractions of the cost of name brands. Delivered assembled. Multiple upgrades available.

Do your ramps hold up outside?

With our seal, stain, or paint upgrades — yes. Wood and weather don't mix, so we recommend at least sealing if your ramp lives outdoors.
